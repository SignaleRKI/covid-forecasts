Acknowledgements

The effort parallels the US COVID-19 forecast hub run by the UMass-Amherst Influenza Forecasting Center of Excellence based at the Reich Lab. Many technical choices and software have been taken over from this effort. We would like to thank the Reich Lab Team for their generous support and the fruitful collaboration.

We would like to thank Michal Rogalski for aggregating Polish regional-level data and making them available in a machine-readable form.

The Forecast Hub project is part of the SIMCARD Information& Data Science Pilot Project funded by the Helmholtz Association. We moreover wish to acknowledge the Alexander von Humboldt Foundation whose support facilitated early interactions and collaboration with the Reich Lab.
